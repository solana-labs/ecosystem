Scallop is a scallop growing simulation game on Solana with a user-funded pooled treasury, developed by three young engineers who love ocean across the globe. Users can stake theirs SOL here to grow a scallop and have chance to win extra rewards. At the end of each week, one (or a few) of random winner will share out 100% of equivalence price of the tank's SOL reward's Scallop token (SCA), 93% of SOL reward will be SCA buyback, 7% will back to the tank.
